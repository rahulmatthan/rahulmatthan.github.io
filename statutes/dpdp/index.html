Digital Personal Data Protection

The DPDP Act, 2023 and the DPDP Rules, 2025 — navigable.

A structured, cross-referenced edition of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Act No. 22 of 2023) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025 (G.S.R. 846(E), 13 November 2025) made by the Central Government under section 40 of the Act. Each section of the Act lists the Rules made under it; each Rule links back to its enabling section.

What this is

The DPDP Act and Rules are published as PDFs only. This site is a navigable HTML edition of those PDFs, modelled on legislation.gov.uk. Part of the Indian statutes portal; sister site for the Aadhaar corpus at /statutes/aadhaar.
