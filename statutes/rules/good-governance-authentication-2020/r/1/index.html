The Aadhaar Authentication for Good Governance (Social Welfare, Innovation, Knowledge) Rules, 2020

Rule 1 Short title and commencement

as enacted · gazette 2020-08-05

(1) These rules may be called the Aadhaar Authentication for Good Governance (Social Welfare, Innovation, Knowledge) Rules, 2020.

(2) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
