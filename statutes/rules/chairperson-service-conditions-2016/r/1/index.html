The Unique Identification Authority of India (Terms and Conditions of Service of Chairperson and Members) Rules, 2016

Rule 1 Short title and commencement

as enacted · gazette 2016-07-12

(1) These rules may be called the Unique Identification Authority of India (Terms and Conditions of Service of Chairperson and Members) Rules, 2016.

(2) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
