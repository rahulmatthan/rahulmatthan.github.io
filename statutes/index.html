Indian statutes — navigable.

Navigable, cross-referenced editions of Indian statutes and the rules and regulations made under them. Modelled on legislation.gov.uk: section-level pages, verbatim amendment history, and links from each section to the subordinate legislation made under it.

Available

  • Aadhaar Act, 2016

    The Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016, with verbatim amendment history per section, eight rule sets and seven regulation sets, plus every amending instrument from 2019 to 2025.

    59 sections · 5 rule sets · 8 regulation sets · 17 amending instruments

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

    The DPDP Act, 2023 (Act No. 22 of 2023) and the DPDP Rules, 2025 (G.S.R. 846(E), 13 November 2025) made by the Central Government under section 40 of the Act.

    44 sections · 23 rules
