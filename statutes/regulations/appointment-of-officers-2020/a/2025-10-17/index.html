UIDAI (Appointment of Officers and Employees) Third Amendment Regulations, 2025

2025-10-17 · Source PDF · amends Unique Identification Authority of India (Appointment of Officers and Employees) Regulations, 2020

This amending instrument records 5 discrete changes to the Schedule: two adjustments to the deputation-eligibility criteria for Senior Accounts Officer (adding M. Com to the qualifications list, and adding seven-years-of-commerce-graduate as an alternative ISTM-training equivalent), and three sanctioned-strength revisions for Private Secretary (25 → 22), Section Officer (74 → 75) and Assistant Account Officer (10 → 12).
