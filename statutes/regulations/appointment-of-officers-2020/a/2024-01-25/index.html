Unique Identification Authority of India (Appointment of Officers and Employees) Amendment Regulations, 2024

2024-01-25 · Source PDF · amends Unique Identification Authority of India (Appointment of Officers and Employees) Regulations, 2020

This amending instrument records 3 discrete changes to the Schedule: revisions to the sanctioned strength of Private Secretary (40 → 38) and Section Officer (59 → 61), and the addition of contractual mode of recruitment for Assistant Technical Officer.
