Unique Identification Authority of India (Appointment of Officers and Employees) Regulations, 2020 · II — Methods Of Recruitment

Regulation 8 Permanent Absorption after Cadre Constitution

as enacted · gazette 2020-08-06

The Appointing Authority may, from time to time, by appropriate order, appoint on permanent absorption basis any person already appointed and holding any post listed in the Schedule in the Authority and follow such conditions and procedure as provided in sub-regulation (2) to

(4) of regulation 5.
