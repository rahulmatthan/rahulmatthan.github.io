Amending regulation

Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Second Amendment Regulations, 2025

2025-08-22 · Source PDF · amends Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016

This amending instrument records 6 discrete changes to the principal regulations, covering deactivation on death, deactivation of mixed-biometric Aadhaars, removal of the “temporarily” qualifier in regulation 28(2), a new proviso in regulation 30(1), and a wholesale replacement of Form V in Schedule I. The substituted form body is too long to inline — see the source PDF.

2025-08-22 · inserted — regulation 28, sub-regulation (1), after sub-clause (e) Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Second Amendment Regulations, 2025
After

(ea) (i) Where the Authority receives information regarding the death of an Aadhaar number holder including the Aadhaar number of the deceased individual, wherever available, the said information shall be verified in a manner as prescribed by the Authority, and upon such verification, the Aadhaar number shall be deactivated. (ii) In order to ensure continued accuracy of information in the CIDR or to verify the information pertaining to the death of the Aadhar number holder received under sub-regulation (i), the Authority: A. shall seek information from the Registrar General of India or Chief Registrar for a State appointed under the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969; or B. may seek information from any other entity in the Aadhaar ecosystem; or C. may receive information from any individual.
